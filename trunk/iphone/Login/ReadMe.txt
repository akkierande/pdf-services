gDocs
=====
gDox simplifies gdocs access and management. 

Features: 
------ 
- Multiple gmail accounts supported. 
- Password stored in keychain of iphone so its secure. 
- Simple interface 
- More interesting feature soon 
- This app will be free and has an Ad only in the first screen. 

The ad revenue generated will be used to maintain this app. 

Please leave your feedback. 
Thank you.
